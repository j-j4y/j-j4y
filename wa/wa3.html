The World of Winx

Winx Club is an animated television series co-produced by Rainbow SpA and later Nickelodeon. It was created and directed by Italian animator Iginio Straffi. The show premiered on 28 January 2004 and has a total of 8 seasons. The show is set in a magical universe that is inhabited by fairies, witches, and other mythical creatures. The main character is a fairy warrior named Bloom, who enrolls at Alfea College to train and hone her skills. She there meets the rest of the Winx; Stella, Musa, Tecna, Aisha, and Flora
